üMed Guided Intake — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

üMed Guided Intake ("the app") is designed so that your information never leaves your

device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no server, no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no

third-party SDKs that collect data.

How your answers are handled

Your answers to the app's questions are processed entirely on your device, in memory,

to display a result and an optional printable summary. They are not transmitted

anywhere and are discarded when you close or restart the app.

Photos

If a version of the app offers to attach a photo, the photo stays on your device and

appears only in your own printable summary. It is never uploaded.

External links

The app may link to third-party websites (for example, printable vision charts). Those

sites have their own privacy policies; visiting them is your choice.

Health information

The app is an educational demonstration, is not a medical device, and does not

diagnose or treat any condition. Because no data is collected or transmitted, no

health information about you is stored by us.

Children

The app is not directed at children and is intended for adults.

Changes

If a future version changes any of the above, this policy and the app listing will be

updated before that version is released.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact the publisher listed on the App Store page.
